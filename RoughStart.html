What We Do & Who We Are!

This website is an extension of our facebook group, Team 4GP. What we do is, well, code! The page started with four of us. We used it to share CS resources, articles and organize our ideas for hackathons. We expanded with the hope of being able to bring in more resources and we have accomplished just that! This site is will grow over time and help more people learn about what CS is and tools to expand their knowledge and even help give beginners a start.
